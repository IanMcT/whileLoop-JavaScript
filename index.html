Summation with while loops

Summation is the adding of a sequence of numbers. $$\sum\limits_{i = m}^n {a_i}=a_m+a_{m+1}+...a_{n-1}+a_n$$ For example: $$\sum\limits_{i = 1}^4 {i}=1+2+3+4$$ or $$\sum\limits_{i = 1}^4 {i}=10$$

We can use a while loop to solve for $$n$$ for the largest summation that is smaller than x:

$$\sum\limits_{i = 1}^n {i}=x$$
